Who are we?

OUR HISTORY

After working on voluntarily basis in different communities the co-founders:
Zaina Dorcas Ronelle, Dieudonne Kimonge, Emile Nshimiyimana, and Erick Obedi separately thought of collaborating by coming up with an organization that will help to curb the indifference taking place in the communities by creating ways that will help this generation to meet their need without compromising the future generation to meet their needs too and also to ensure the equal contribution of resources. As of today, we have established institutions that provide leadership skills, financial help, and other talking actions in protecting the environment and underprivileged people to improve their living standards.

WHAT WE DO

Creating ways of mitigating climate change, providing sustainable ways of attaining selfreliance, and provision of leadership skills.

HOW WE DO IT

Through campaign and civic education on climate change and leadership issues. We also organize the population to take action such as planting trees, cleaning the environment, and using renewable sources of energy.
